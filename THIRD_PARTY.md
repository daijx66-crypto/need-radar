# Third-party data and attribution

Need Radar links to third-party material; it does not claim ownership of that
material. Titles and short excerpts remain subject to the original publishers'
terms and copyright.

## AI HOT

- Source: <https://aihot.virxact.com/>
- Integration: anonymous read-only `/api/public/*` endpoints.
- Public cards retain `AI HOT` attribution, canonical links, and source links
  when supplied by the API.
- AI HOT content and summaries may contain errors. Verify important claims at
  the original source.

## Follow Builders

- Source: <https://github.com/zarazhangrui/follow-builders>
- Integration: public generated feeds for X, blogs, and podcasts.
- Public cards retain `Follow Builders` attribution and link to the original
  post or episode.

Other collectors are documented in `README.md`. A collector being available
does not grant permission to republish full third-party content; public output
is therefore limited to derived metadata, short excerpts, and links.
